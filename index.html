About Our Firm

Why Choose Us

750+ Projects Delivered

With a legacy built on precision, integrity, and innovation, TECHKNOW Consultants and Constructions (TCC) is a multi-disciplinary consultancy led by Mr. Sreedhar Kakarala (M.Tech, Industrial Structures — SJCE Mysore), specializing in architectural planning, structural design, and project management. From the intricate spiritual requirements of religious structures to the high-stakes demands of industrial engineering, we bring a wealth of experience across 750+ completed projects.

Our approach blends traditional wisdom, such as Vastu Shastra, with modern engineering excellence. As proud members of CII-IGBC and certified experts in the Energy Conservation Building Code (ECBC), we deliver spaces that are not only structurally sound but also harmoniously designed and environmentally responsible.

Diverse Industry Footprint

Expertise across 15+ industrial verticals — we understand the specific technical requirements of various manufacturing and commercial sectors.

Scale & Reliability

Having managed over 500 residential projects and 50+ PMCs, we have the refined systems in place to handle projects of any scale.

End-to-End Solutions

We don't just plan; we execute. From the first blueprint to the final civil handover, we provide a single point of accountability.

Specialized Expertise

We are one of the few firms with a dedicated portfolio of 30+ religious structures, requiring a unique blend of cultural sensitivity and structural complexity.

Quality & Precision

Our commitment to structural integrity ensures that every school, convention center, and layout we design is built to last for generations.
