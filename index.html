About Our Firm

Why Choose Us

600+ Projects Delivered

With a legacy built on precision, integrity, and innovation, TECHKNOW Consultants and Constructions (TCC) is a multi-disciplinary consultancy led by Mr. Sreedhar Kakarala (M.Tech, Industrial Structures — SJCE Mysore), specializing in architectural planning, structural design, and project management. From the intricate spiritual requirements of religious structures to the high-stakes demands of industrial engineering, we bring a wealth of experience across 600+ completed projects.

Our approach blends traditional wisdom, such as Vastu Shastra, with modern engineering excellence. As proud members of CII-IGBC and certified experts in the Energy Conservation Building Code (ECBC), we deliver spaces that are not only structurally sound but also harmoniously designed and environmentally responsible.

Proven Expertise

Led by Sridhar Kakarala, M.Tech in Industrial Structures, with over 35 years of hands‑on experience in structural design, construction project management, and industrial facilities — from oil and rice mills to ayurvedic production units and large residential complexes.

Comprehensive Services

End‑to‑end capability covering civil engineering design, construction management, industrial structures, factory setups, conventions and residential projects, plus specialized Vastu consultancy to align design with client goals.

Industry‑Specific Knowledge

Deep experience in sector‑critical standards and workflows (oil, food processing, pharmaceuticals/ayurvedic, industrial plants), ensuring safe, compliant and efficient facility design and construction.

Quality & Safety First

Rigorous quality control, engineering best practices, and safety protocols at every project phase to minimize risk, reduce rework, and protect people and assets.

Efficient Project Delivery

Strong project management skills, schedule discipline and resource coordination to meet timelines and budgets while maintaining high workmanship standards.

Client‑Centric Approach

Tailored solutions that reflect each client's functional, financial and aesthetic requirements; clear communication, transparent reporting and responsive on‑site support.

Sustainable & Cost‑Effective Solutions

Practical designs that optimize materials, reduce life‑cycle costs and incorporate sustainable practices where feasible — delivering long‑term value.

Proven Track Record

Decades of successful projects across residential, industrial and commercial sectors, backed by repeat clients and practical experience executing complex structural and process‑driven facilities.

Choose TechknowCC for experienced leadership, sector expertise, reliable delivery and engineering solutions that work — from concept through commissioning.
